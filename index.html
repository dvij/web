I am a research scientist at DeepMind. I'm interested in building verifiable and secure AI systems, that come with rigorous guarantees on their worst case behavior. In this effort, I draw on tools from convex optimization, control theory . More generally, I am interested in developing algorithms for reasoning about complex autonomous systems in a rigorous manner. In past work, I developed tools for analyzing electric power grids with high penetration of uncertain renewable energy sources like wind and solar energy.
